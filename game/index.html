    How to Play

    • Move — Arrow Keys (or WASD)
    • Shoot arrow — Space — tap for a quick shot, or hold to charge for more damage & range, then release to fire
    • Aim the shot — hold Space + Arrow Keys to gradually sweep your aim toward that direction; release the arrow key to hold the angle (you stand still while drawing the bow)
    • Dash — Shift while moving (quick dodge)
    • Melee slash — F (fast, short range)

    Drain the enemy's health bar to win. Different fighters and arenas change speed, damage, and the obstacles you fight around.
